VibeCheck

Real-time team sentiment intelligence that helps managers catch burnout before it happens and build healthier work cultures.

The Problem

Managers can't feel the room anymore. Remote teams hide burnout behind "I'm fine" messages. Annual surveys are too late. By the time someone quits, the damage is done.

The Solution

VibeCheck passively analyzes communication patterns (never content!) to surface team health signals. Anonymous pulse checks, trend alerts, and AI-suggested interventions help leaders act early.
