RegenDAO

A decentralized organization funding and verifying environmental regeneration projects through community governance and satellite data.

The Problem

Climate funding is opaque, slow, and centralized. Local regeneration projects struggle to get funded, and donors can't verify impact. Greenwashing erodes trust in environmental claims.

The Solution

RegenDAO lets communities propose, fund, and verify regeneration projects on-chain. Satellite imagery + on-ground sensors provide real-time proof of impact. Token holders govern treasury allocation.
